Our rugs are made from a premium cotton blend with a thick 3cm pile depth. The fibers are hypoallergenic, ultra-soft, and gentle on sensitive skin — perfect for babies, children, and pets. The backing is high-quality rubber for non-slip safety.

Each rug measures 50cm wide × 120cm long (approximately 20" × 47"). This is the perfect "runner" size — ideal for beside a bed, in a bathroom, along a hallway, or in front of a sofa.

The rug is easy to maintain:
• Spot clean: Dab gently with mild soap and water
• Machine wash: Use cold/warm water on gentle cycle
• Air dry: Lay flat or hang to dry — avoid tumble drying
